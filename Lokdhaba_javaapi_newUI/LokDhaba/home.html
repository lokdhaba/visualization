Lokdhaba Food For Political Thought
The Centre conducts real time data collections on ongoing General and Assembly Elections, and diffuses data driven analysis through print and electronic media. The coverage includes the analysis and contextualisation of results and the profiling of the main party candidates. The project aims at measuring individual incumbency in General and State Elections.It reequires the coding of incumbency, The quantification of turncoats and defections, and candidates name matching.
